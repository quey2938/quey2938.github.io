What We Do

Objectives

promote academic growth in the field of Computer Science by encouraging collaboration among students. ACSS is dedicated to creating opportunities for its members, supporting both their academic and professional development, and building connections with fellow students, alumni, and industry professionals.

Goals

build a strong and welcoming community where Computer Studies students can connect, support each other, and grow together both inside and outside the classroom.

provide workshops, seminars, and activities that help students improve their technical skills, stay updated with industry trends, and prepare for future careers.

create fun and meaningful events that motivate students to participate, showcase their talents, and develop leadership and teamwork along the way.

ACSS is for Students

designed to provide a platform for computer science students to grow academically, professionally, and personally. It connects students with resources and opportunities to help them succeed.

Advocacy

for digital literacy and innovation. Encourages students to develop a passion for technology, coding, and innovation, while working to create equal opportunities within STEM fields.
