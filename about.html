History

ACSS's First General Assembly

Association of Computer Studies Students started with its first General Assembly held on September 15, 2010, at UE-Caloocan MPH1 En. Bldg. That day was really special because it marked the start of the organization and the history that would follow for ACSS. The first assembly wasn’t just about talks or introductions — it was also filled with fun and excitement. Students joined not only to meet new faces and learn what ACSS is all about.
